FAQs

So why Yime?

As we get busier, our responsiblities tend to increase exponentially. As a result, we tend to drift apart from our close friends. Most of the time it's due to simple logistical problems rather than relationship problems. By eliminating the need to memorize everyone's schedule, Yime makes it easier to maintain your social life, and precious friendships.

What happens next?

We're planning a beta launch with limited users to refine our app. Once you submit your email for an invite, we will eventually invite you to participate. Meanwhile, if you like the concept then let us know, and invite your friends to do the same!

Why not Google Calendar?

Yime makes it a lot easier to input your schedule. While Google Calendar is great, it is not as simple as Yime. Think of Yime as an inverse of Google calendar, but a lot easier.

Is it free?

Yes. For the forseeable future it's free.
